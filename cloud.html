Cloudiness



There is no correlation between latitude and cloudiness (North: 22%, South: 28%) in both hemispheres. Cloudiness tends to be linked more to land or ocean cover, with cloudiness being higher over oceans vs. land. Since land mass and ocean coverage is not related to latitude, it makes sense that there is no relationship between latitude and cloudiness.
